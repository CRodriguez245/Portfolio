About Me

I'm Carlos Rodriguez, a design strategist and product thinker working at the intersection of healthcare, education, and human-centered AI.

With a background in computer science and a graduate education in design strategy from the Institute of Design, I've spent the last few years designing programs, research tools, and systems that empower people—especially those historically left out of innovation conversations. Whether it's using AI to boost research confidence for first-gen students, embedding Microsoft Copilot into healthcare education, or mapping systemic breakdowns in cohort-based programs, I bring a strategic lens to complex challenges.

My work often lives in the gray space between structure and creativity:

  • Designing frameworks that scale with integrity
  • Facilitating cross-disciplinary teams toward shared clarity
  • Prototyping experiences where tools like AI become more human, not less

I care deeply about building futures where technology supports—not replaces—our judgment, relationships, and growth. If you're working on something that blends equity, systems, and strategy, I'd love to hear from you.
